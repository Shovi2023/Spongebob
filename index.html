SpongeBob SquarePants is a famous show for kids growing up from 1999 to now, it has many funny, interesting, and nostalgic memories attached to it. This show brings back so many memories for everyone but for me, I always thought it was funny to see how many times Spongebob cries in the show. This site will document the times Spongebob cried from 1999 to 2002, and these will be displayed with illustrations I drew myself from those corresponding episodes.
